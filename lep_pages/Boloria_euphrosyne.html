Common name

Deutscher Name
Silberfleck-Perlmuttfalter
Family
Nymphalidae
Family common name
Brush-footed Butterflies
On the wing

Habitat
Nutrient-poor, bushy grasslands often near the woods, fringes and clearcuts in the forest and forest gap systems.
Larval host:
Viola species such as Viola hirta and Viola reichenbachiana.
