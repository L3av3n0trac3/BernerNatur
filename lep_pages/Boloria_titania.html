Boloria titania

Common name
Titania's Fritillary
Deutscher Name
Natterwurz-Perlmuttfalter (Adder's-wort Fritillary)
Family
Nymphalidae
Family common name
Brush-footed Butterflies
On the wing
L June to August.
Habitat
Forest gap systems, fens and other wetlands near forests and mountainous meadows and pastures that are interspersed with spruce trees.
Larval host:
Viola species (especially Viola bicolor) and Polygonum bistorta.
