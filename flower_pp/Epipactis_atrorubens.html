Epipactis atrorubens

Common name
Dark-red Helleborine
Deutscher Name
Dunkelrote Stendelwurz
Family
Orchidaceae
Family common name
Orchid family
Blooms
L May - E September
Habitat
Warm and dry locations, with soil basic to neutral in pH, nutrient-poor, and permeable. Loose rock, scree, or sandy soils above a limestone substrate, including dunes, lawns, or open forest. Among communities of grass and bush and light birch stands. To 2,400 m (7,900 ft).
